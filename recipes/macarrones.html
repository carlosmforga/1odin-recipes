Macaroni Bolognese Recipe

Description

Macaroni Bolognese is a pasta dish that combines macaroni with a minced meat and tomato sauce. The sauce, originating from Bologna, is slowly cooked with onion, carrot, celery, red wine, and spices to enhance the flavor.

Once the sauce is ready, it is mixed with the al dente cooked macaroni and served with grated Parmesan cheese. It’s a simple and tasty recipe, ideal for enjoying a pasta dish with rich texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Onion
  • Red wine
  • Carrot
  • Olive oil
  • Minced meat (choose between beef and pork)
  • Crushed tomato
  • Celery
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps to follow

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the chopped onion, carrot, and celery in olive oil over medium heat until tender.
  3. Add the minced meat, cook until browned and release its juices.
  4. Pour in the red wine and let it reduce to evaporate the alcohol.
  5. Add the crushed tomato, bay leaf, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30-40 minutes to intensify the flavors.
  6. Mix the sauce with the cooked macaroni and stir well.
  7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
