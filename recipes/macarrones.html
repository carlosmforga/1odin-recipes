Macaroni Bolognese Recipe

Description

Macaroni Bolognese is a pasta dish that combines macaroni with a minced meat and tomato sauce. The sauce, originating from Bologna, is slowly cooked with onion, carrot, celery, red wine, and spices to enhance the flavor.

Once the sauce is ready, it is mixed with the al dente cooked macaroni and served with grated Parmesan cheese. Itâ€™s a simple and tasty recipe, ideal for enjoying a pasta dish with rich texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Onion
  • Red wine
  • Carrot
  • Olive oil
  • Minced meat (choose between beef and pork)
  • Crushed tomato
  • Celery
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Parmesan cheese
