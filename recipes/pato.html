Duck Breast Recipe

Description

Magret de pato is a dish made with the breast of a duck raised for foie gras, characterized by its juicy meat and a layer of fat that browns when cooked. It is prepared by scoring the skin in a diamond pattern and cooking it over medium heat, allowing the fat to melt and form a crispy crust while the inside remains pink and tender.

Once cooked, the magret is sliced thinly and is often served with sweet-and-sour sauces, such as red wine reduction, red fruits, or orange. It is a refined and tasty dish, where the balance between juicy meat and crispy skin makes it an exquisite option to enjoy an elegant and flavorful preparation.

Ingredients

  • Magret de pato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red wine
  • Honey
  • Red fruits
  • Orange
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
